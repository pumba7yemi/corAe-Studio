# corAe Source Governance Law (SGL-01)

This law governs ALL content sources used inside corAe OS² (Home, Work, Business, Faith, Courses, Health, Legal, Finance, HR, OS manuals).

No module, wizard, agent, or workflow is allowed to pull content from ungoverned or low-trust sources.

---

## 1. Tiers of Sources

All sources used inside corAe are classified into TIERS.

### Tier 0 — corAe Primary (Internal)

- Subject 1 testimony and direct instructions.
- corAe OS² official manuals, policies, and governance docs.
- Client-uploaded official documents (contracts, SOPs, policies, licences, manuals).
- Data created and approved inside corAe, marked as **corAe Checked**.
